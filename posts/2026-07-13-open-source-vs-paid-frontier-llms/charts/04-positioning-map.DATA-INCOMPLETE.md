intelligence. Open-weight models occupy the value zone (capable and cheap);
paid flagships occupy the premium tier (top of the chart, high price)."

Unlike charts 01-03, this image is not described with any individual
model's numeric coordinates at all — only the two zone-level qualitative
statements above. Building an actual price/intelligence 2x2 map requires a
price and an index score per model, and (per the DATA-INCOMPLETE notes for
charts 01-03 in this same folder) most of the ten models named in this post
do not have both numbers stated in the post text. No generator was written
because there is nothing more specific than "open = value zone, paid =
premium tier" to plot, and placing individual dots would mean inventing
their positions.
